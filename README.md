## **Project Title**

**Hospital Drugstore Management & Inter-Hospital Resource Sharing Platform**

---

## **Project Objective**

To design and build a web-based coordination and intelligence platform that improves the way hospitals:

* Manage pharmacy inventory
* Predict shortages and wastage
* Share critical drugs, blood units, and organs
* Coordinate during emergencies

The platform works alongside existing Hospital Management Systems — not as a replacement — and focuses on visibility, communication, and decision support.
